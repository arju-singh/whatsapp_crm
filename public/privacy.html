Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 June 2026 · WhatsApp CRM · Mumbai, India

Template notice: This is a good-faith starting draft. Before public launch, have it reviewed by qualified legal counsel for your jurisdiction (India DPDP Act 2023, EU/UK GDPR, and any others that apply to your users).

1. Who we are

WhatsApp CRM ("we", "us", "the Service") is a customer-relationship tool that helps businesses manage WhatsApp and email outreach. We are based in Mumbai, India. For any privacy question, contact connect@akshaykotish.com.

2. Data we collect

We collect data you provide and data generated while you use the Service:

CategoryExamples
Account dataName, phone number, email address, hashed password, role.
Customer/contact data you importNames, phone numbers, emails, companies, notes, message and call history.
Usage & analyticsPage views, feature events, IP address, browser/user-agent, timestamps.
BillingSubscription status and plan. Card details are handled by Stripe — we never store them.
SupportMessages you send via contact or bug-report forms.

3. How your data is stored

The Service stores your account and CRM data in a database on your own hosted server. We use WhatsApp Web (via an automated browser session) to send and receive messages; we are not Meta's official Cloud API and do not route your customer database through Meta. If your WhatsApp number is disconnected or banned, your stored CRM data remains intact on your server.

4. How we use data

  • To operate the Service — authenticate you, send/receive messages, run campaigns and follow-ups.
  • To provide analytics and reporting back to you.
  • To process subscriptions and prevent abuse (rate limiting, fraud checks).
  • To respond to your support requests.

5. Third-party processors

ProviderPurpose

Your SMTP/email providerSending transactional and campaign email.

6. Cookies

We use a strictly-necessary session cookie to keep you signed in, and first-party analytics to understand usage. See our Cookie Policy for details and choices.

7. Data retention

We keep account and CRM data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time (see §8). Analytics events are retained in aggregate for product improvement.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise them, email connect@akshaykotish.com. EU/UK users may also lodge a complaint with their supervisory authority.

9. Security

Passwords are hashed (scrypt). Access is gated by authenticated sessions and role-based controls. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your data.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
