The instruments of the trade, quietly remarkable.

Six tools, each doing one thing well. Together they replace the messy stack of spreadsheets, second phones, and forgotten promises that most WhatsApp sellers run on.

№ 01 — Outbound

Bulk messaging, with manners

Send personalized WhatsApp messages to hundreds of contacts at a pace WhatsApp's anti-abuse systems quietly approve of. Variables fill in. Failures retry. You don't get banned.

№ 02 — Cadence

Automatic follow-ups

"If no reply in 48 hours, send Template B." Build sequences that pause the moment a prospect responds, and resume when interest reignites. Sleep through the work.

№ 03 — Enrichment

Profile pictures & bios

Pull each contact's avatar, About text, and business flag from WhatsApp directly. Cached locally — links never expire. Your CRM finally looks like the chat app it was born from.

№ 04 — Pipeline

Stages, deals, calendar

Drag-and-drop pipeline, deal scoring, calendar of every touchpoint. Everything a real CRM has — built lean and editable, no expensive seats.

№ 05 — Inbox

One place for replies

WhatsApp and email responses funnel into a single thread per contact. Quick-reply from your browser. Read receipts roll in. Never lose a hot lead in a notification flood again.

№ 06 — Intelligence

AI drafts, you decide

Optional AI suggests responses tailored to each conversation's tone and history. You review and approve — nothing fires without your finger on the trigger.

ii.

Import your contacts

Pull every saved contact in one click. Or upload a CSV. Avatars and About text enrich themselves in the background.

Compose a campaign. Queue follow-ups. Replies land in the inbox. The pipeline updates itself when a deal moves.
